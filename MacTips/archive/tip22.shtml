Tip #22, Save the Clones!

This week's MacTip is a little off the normal subject but it is none the less vitally important to the future of the Mac OS.

If you are new to MacTips please check out the MacTip Archive to see previous MacTips.

The "Save the Clones!" campaign was created Tim Dunton and I to provide Apple feedback from the millions of Mac OS users indicating their support for the Mac clone market. Our petition is simply for Apple to continue broad-based licensing of the Mac OS in a manner which isn't prohibitive to clone manufacturers.

Tip #22 apeared August 12th 1997.
